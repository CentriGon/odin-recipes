Peanut Butter Jelly Recipe

Epic Peanut Butter Jelly sandwich :coolemoji:

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Second Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly
  • Hands (For making, not eating)
  • 2 dishes (not for eating either)
  • Mouth (Does not have to be yours)

Procedure

  1. Take the first bread and place it on a dish with hands
  2. Take Peanut butter and place it on top of bread with hands
  3. Take second bread and place it on a dish that is not the first one with your hands
  4. Take the jelly and place it on the second piece of bread (the one without the peanut butter) with your hands
  5. Place the two pieces of peanut butter and jelly on top of each other (the jelly side must touch the peanut butter side else it will be not as good) with your hands
  6. Put the Peanut butter Jelly sandwich inside of the mouth with your hands
  7. The end
